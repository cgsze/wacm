About

Who We Are

WACM is the University of Wisconsin-Madison's student chapter of ACM-W, ACM's Women in Computing. We provide social, educational and outreach activities for women in the Computer Sciences department, in the university, and in the surrounding community.


How to Contact Us

For inquiries about events, collaborations, or workshops, please contact our co-presidents, Cathy Cao and Kayley Seow. For questions related to specific committees, please reach out directly to the respective committee chairs.
